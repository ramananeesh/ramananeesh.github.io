About

I am a PhD student at Boston University and a member of the DiSC Lab, working with Prof. Manos Athanassoulis. My research focus is on Database systems - indexing and access methods, and my interests include high performance computing, data mining and machine learning. Prior to Boston University, I was at Purdue University Fort Wayne, where I graduated with highest distinction with a Bachelor's in Computer Science. There, I worked with Prof. Zesheng Chen on my senior capstone project.
