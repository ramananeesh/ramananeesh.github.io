Hi! I am a PhD candidate at Boston University adivsed by Prof. Manos Athanassoulis. My research focuses on Database systems, particularly indexing and access methods. Recently, I have been working on instance-optimized indexes that harness inherent data order or sortedness to improve performance.

Before joining Boston University, I attended Purdue University Fort Wayne for my bachelors degree.

I am currently on the job market, looking for full-time opportunities in the industry.
